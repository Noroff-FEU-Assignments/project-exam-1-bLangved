What is Guitarlicious?

Welcome to Guitarlicious, a blog dedicated to the love of all things guitar. Our website is the ultimate destination for guitar enthusiasts, whether you're a professional musician or a beginner just starting out. We cover everything from the latest gear and gadgets to the most iconic players and performances, with a focus on helping you become the best guitar player you can be. Our team of expert writers brings you the latest news, reviews, and tutorials on all things guitar-related. We strive to provide you with the most comprehensive and informative content available, so you can stay up-to-date on the latest trends and developments in the world of guitar. Our mission is simple: to inspire, educate, and entertain you with our love for this incredible instrument. So whether you're looking for advice on buying your first guitar or you're a seasoned pro seeking new tips and tricks, Guitarlicious is here to help you on your musical journey. Join us today and discover the true meaning of Guitarlicious!

What is the goal with the blog?

It's important to note that Guitarlicious is a non-profit blog. We do not receive any funding or financial support for our content, and we do this purely out of our love for the guitar. We are a group of passionate musicians and writers who want to share our knowledge and experience with others who share our passion. Our goal is to provide a platform for guitar enthusiasts to connect, learn, and grow together. We believe that music has the power to bring people together, and the guitar is one of the most beautiful instruments that can create a sense of community and belonging. So, whether you're a beginner or a seasoned player, we welcome you to Guitarlicious and hope that our content will inspire you to continue exploring and enjoying this wonderful instrument.

Want to contribute?

Do you share our love for the guitar and want to contribute to our mission of spreading guitar knowledge and passion? We are always on the lookout for talented writers who are passionate about the guitar and want to share their insights and experiences with our readers. Whether you're a professional musician, a gear expert, or just a dedicated hobbyist, we welcome your unique perspective and ideas. By becoming a writer for Guitarlicious, you'll have the opportunity to showcase your skills, build your portfolio, and connect with a community of like-minded guitar enthusiasts. If you're interested in contributing, please reach out to us through our contact page. We'd love to hear from you and see how we can work together to create even more amazing guitar content for our readers.
